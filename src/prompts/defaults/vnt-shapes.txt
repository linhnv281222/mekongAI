PHAN LOAI HINH DANG & KIEU PHOI:
Phi tron dac: tron xoay dac, truc bac,ulong,chot,bac dac → Tien CNC, phoi thanh tron
Phi tron ong: tron xoay co lo xuyen tâm lớn >30% ĐK ngoài, bac rong, vong → Tien CNC, phoi ong
Hinh tam: khoi hop/tam phang, cao <50% dai, de/bich/block → Phay CNC, phoi tam
Luc giac: phoi luc giac, dau ulong luc giac → Tien CNC
Hon hop: vua tron xoay vua mat phang phuc tap → Tien + Phay
